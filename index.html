COMMON QUESTIONS

Before you submit an enquiry

Do you guarantee loan approval?

No. Only the lender can approve or reject a loan after credit, income, legal, technical and policy checks.

Do you charge customers?

Initial consultation is free. Any customer-payable service fee, if applicable, must be disclosed in writing before engagement.

Should I upload PAN, Aadhaar or bank statements here?

No. This demo website intentionally avoids document uploads. Share documents only through a verified and secure channel after confirming the recipient.

Will checking eligibility affect my credit score?

This website’s calculator does not access your credit report. A formal lender enquiry may be recorded according to the credit bureau’s process.
