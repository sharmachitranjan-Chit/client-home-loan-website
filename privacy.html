Privacy Notice

Last updated: 11 July 2026

This template must be reviewed and updated with the DSA’s legal name, address, contact details, actual data practices and lender arrangements before publication.

Information we collect

The enquiry form may collect your name, mobile number, city, requested product, approximate loan requirement, employment category and message. The form prepares a WhatsApp message; it does not upload identity or financial documents to this website.

How information is used

Information is used to respond to your enquiry, understand your broad requirement, coordinate with you, and where you specifically agree, assist in submitting an application to an appropriate regulated lender.

What not to submit

Do not enter Aadhaar numbers, PAN numbers, bank account numbers, card details, passwords, OTPs or document images in the public enquiry form.

Sharing

Personal information should be shared with banks, housing finance companies, NBFCs, credit bureaus, verification agencies or service providers only when necessary for the requested service, with appropriate authority or consent, and according to applicable law and the relevant lender’s process.

Retention and security

Enquiry records should be retained only as long as needed for legitimate business, legal or compliance purposes. Reasonable safeguards should be used, but no internet transmission is completely risk-free.

Your choices

You may request correction or deletion of enquiry information, subject to legal and record-retention obligations. Add the client’s grievance contact here before launch.
